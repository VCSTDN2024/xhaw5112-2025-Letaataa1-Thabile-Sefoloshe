Company Description: 

Empowering the Nation is a registered small to mid-sized enterprise established by Ms. Precious Radebe with the objective of providing structured skill development training for domestic workers and gardeners. This initiative was founded as a response to the limited accessibility to formal education and upskilling opportunities experienced by several individuals within Ms. Radebe’s community.
This enterprise is dedicated to enhancing the employability and earning potential of domestic workers and gardeners by equipping them with practical and market-relevant skills. Empowering the Nation aims to improve participant’s job prospect, enhance their capacity to negotiate improved remuneration, and enable them to peruse entrepreneurial ventures where applicable.
The enterprise offers two distinct training programmes:
1.	A six-month Learnership which is delivered over a period of twelve weeks.
2.	A Short Skills Training Programmes which is delivered over a period of six-weeks
Both programmes type is available to employees including employers seeking to invest in the professional development of their staff. Clients ae permitted to register for their multiple courses, below is a structured discount scheme:
•	One course – no discount
•	Two courses – 5% discount 
•	Three courses – 10% discount 
•	More than three courses – 15% discount

This report outlines a formal recommendation for the development of a professional web page and mobile application for Empowering the Nation, a small-to mid-sized enterprise based in Johannesburg. 
This proposed digital solution aims to strengthen the organisation’s outreach, streamline the courses registration process and provide a user-friendly platform which consumers can engage with the services offered.

Purpose of Mobile Application & Web Page
The mobile application and web page are intended to fulfil the following strategic objectives:
1.	Advertise available training programmes in a structured and professional manner.
2.	Endorse the enterprise’s visions including goals to an extensive audience.
3.	Facilitate course selection, registration, and quotation generation.
4.	Amplify the credibility and brand image of Empower the Nation through well-considered visual and functional design.

Web Page: Functionality Components & Design Principles
This web page will reflect the professional and empowering character of the organisation. The visual identification will incorporate the newly introduced logo including a carefully curated palette of warm earth tones and a vibrant blues, which symbolise growth, diversity, and trust. 
A pivotal attribute of this design is the logo; it consists of stylized human figures forming the trunk of a flourishing tree. The tree branches and leaves symbolize knowledge, growth, and empowerment which are essential values of the enterprise’s purpose. The logo’s colour scheme plays an imperative role in reinforcing the enterprise’s values:
•	Blue – This colour embodies reliability, trust, and renewal, elicits intellectual development and emotional support.
•	Earth Tones – This colour represents resilience, humbleness, and communication, evokes a reminder of humble yet imperative roles gardeners and domestic workers play.
The design will be carefully responsive to ensure accessibility across all devices, incorporating desktops, tablets, and smartphones.
